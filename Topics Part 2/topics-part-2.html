A Curated Compilation on Adulting & Living in Experiences

Imagine you’re a grown-up, and being a grown-up means you have to do lots of important stuff like going to work, taking care of your home, and being nice to people. That’s called “adulting.” But you know what? Grown-ups also need to have fun and enjoy special moments, like playing with your toys, going to the park, or having ice cream. That's how they return to themselves. That’s called being “present” or being right there in the fun moment.

Sometimes, being a grown-up and having fun moments can be like a seesaw. You have to balance them. If you only do grown-up stuff all the time, it’s not so much fun. And if you only have fun all the time, you might forget important grown-up things. So, being a good grown-up means finding the right balance. You do your grown-up things, but you also make sure to have lots of fun moments and enjoy them like a big kid. That way, Jack won't be a dull boy!
